Hearts Card Game Rules

Classic 4-player Hearts. Goal: take as few points as possible. Below is the clean ruleset (scoring, passing, and trick play).

Scoring

  • Each Heart (♥) = 1 point
  • Queen of Spades (♠Q) = 13 points
  • Lowest total score wins.

Passing Cards

At the start of each hand, each player chooses 3 cards to pass. Direction rotates each hand: Left → Right → Across → No Pass.

Trick Rules

  • One player leads a suit; others must follow suit if possible.
  • If you can’t follow suit, you may play any card.
  • Highest card of the lead suit wins the trick and leads next.

Common House Variations (Optional)

  • Shooting the Moon: taking all points in a hand flips scoring (varies by table).
  • Some tables restrict breaking Hearts until a point card is played.
