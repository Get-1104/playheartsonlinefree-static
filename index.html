How to Play Hearts

Hearts is a trick-taking card game where you want to avoid penalty cards. Each round begins with a card pass, then players follow suit and win tricks based on the highest card in the lead suit. Hearts are worth 1 point each, and the Queen of Spades is worth 13 points. The player with the lowest total score wins.

  • Follow suit if possible; otherwise, play any card.
  • Hearts cannot be led until they are broken.
  • Shooting the moon gives all points to your opponents.
